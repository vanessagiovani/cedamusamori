Game Overview

Cupid encountered Psyche a long time ago, and she made him fall in love with her. Cupid's mother, Venus, is so envious of Psyche's beauty that she curses her to love a man who does not deserve it. After overcoming the several trials that Venus presented to Psyche, she was ultimately allowed to see Cupid again. Now is your time to help Cupid to shoot his arrow!

Play the game and let's see how far can you help him!
